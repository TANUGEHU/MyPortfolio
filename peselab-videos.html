PESE LAB SUMMARY

LAB 1

In today's lab, ma'am observed our gestures, postures, and personalities. At first, she seemed strict, but once she started speaking, I was impressed—especially by her diverse academic background. She also shared some stories, which made the session more engaging. She has a personality that fills everyone with enthusiasm,creates a motivated and positive learning environment—exactly the kind of personality every career skills teacher should possess. Towards the end, we played a really interesting game called Chinese Whisper. She made us stand in a queue, then whispered a phrase to the first person. Each of us had to pass it along to the next, and by the time it reached the last person, the phrase had completely changed!

Key Learning

  • Effective listening skills
  • Miscommunication can lead to big blunders

LAB 2

In today's session ma'am taught us about the outer appearance of a person which is the first impression of a person. She gave some examples where a person's appearance can make or break their chances of success. Then she told us how to make a cv with all necessary details.Then mam conducted an activity where she instructed to tear a page edges accordingly,fold,tear right edge,left edge and so on and again she instructed to take a paper and repeated the same steps and when we opened the pages we saw that both the pages have different patterns but the instructions were same.

Key Learning

  • Our appearance makes a strong impression
  • Active listening is important

LAB 3

In today's session mam told about the importance of soft skills, how soft skills help us to get ahead from others and how it helps in creating positive impact on others. She shared a story which told the importance of soft skills. After that an activity was conducted which showed that how luck, skill, hardwork and right attitude plays important role in our life. Even if you are a hardworking person , you have best luck and money but if you don't have right attitude then you can't achieve success.

Key Learning

  • Right attitude is very important in life

LAB 4

Today mam was really upset as some students cheated in the mid term practical and the students thought that mam would not know about this but they were caught. Mam told that cheating in life would never help us as it would only make us coward not stronger.Then she told about her husband who was fearing to do some task but mam told him not to fear and complete the task and not to make any excuses to avoid that task.She told us to face the challenges not to run away from them.

Key Learning

  • Always face the challenges never run away from them

LAB 5

Today's session was all about Group Discussion. Mam told us how a GD is conducted, told some rules related to it and counducted GD's.Mam told us that the starting of the GD should be impactful and so the concluson. She also told us to keep and pen and paper to jot down the points in GD to remember the key points of the topic. A GD is conducted to know how well we can outshine among different mentality people.

Key Learning

  • team work and collaboration,critical thinking, active listening
